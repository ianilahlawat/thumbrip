What makes a great YouTube title?

A great YouTube title does three things: it contains your main keyword (for SEO), it creates curiosity or promises value (for clicks), and it accurately represents your content (for watch time). All three together lead to YouTube recommending your video more.

Keep titles under 60 characters so they don't get cut off in search results. Put your most important keyword near the beginning. Numbers (like "7 ways" or "in 10 minutes") significantly boost click-through rates — viewers know exactly what they're getting.
