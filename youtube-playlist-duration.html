Why calculate YouTube playlist duration?

Students use this tool to plan study schedules before watching lecture playlists. Professionals use it to estimate how long a course will take. Binge-watchers use it to decide if a playlist fits their evening. It's one of the most practically useful YouTube tools for anyone who watches video content seriously.
