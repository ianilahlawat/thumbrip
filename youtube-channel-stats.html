How to check YouTube channel stats

Knowing a channel's stats helps you research competitors, find collaboration partners, and understand the size of your niche. Creators use channel stats to benchmark their own growth against similar channels.

ThumbRip extracts publicly available channel information that YouTube displays to all visitors. For deeper analytics on your own channel — like watch time, revenue, and audience demographics — use YouTube Studio, which is available to all channel owners for free.
