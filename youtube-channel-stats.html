About YouTube channel statistics

This tool uses the official YouTube Data API v3 to fetch accurate, real-time channel statistics. Data includes subscriber count, total video count, total view count, and account creation date — all publicly available information.

Note: Channels can choose to hide their exact subscriber count on YouTube. In those cases, YouTube returns an approximate count (e.g. "1.2M") rather than the exact number.
