Why check YouTube video stats?

Creators and marketers use video stats to research competitors, understand what content performs well in their niche, and track their own video performance. Seeing a video's basic info helps you gauge its reach and relevance before sharing or collaborating.

For full advanced analytics on your own videos, use YouTube Studio — it provides detailed data on watch time, impressions, and audience retention. ThumbRip's stats viewer is useful for quickly checking any public video's basic information.
