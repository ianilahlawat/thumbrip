FAQ
Common questions.
Everything you need to know about ThumbRip and how it works.
Is ThumbRip completely free?+
Yes — no signup, no subscription, no hidden fees. Use it as many times as you want.
Who uses a YouTube thumbnail downloader?+
YouTubers study competitor thumbnails for inspiration. Designers create templates. Teachers save images for presentations. Marketers track what content performs well. Used by thousands of creators every day.
What thumbnail sizes are available?+
All 5 YouTube thumbnail sizes: Max Resolution (1280×720), Standard HD (640×480), High Quality (480×360), Medium Quality (320×180), and Small (120×90).
Does it work with YouTube Shorts?+
Yes — ThumbRip works with regular YouTube videos, Shorts, and any public URL format including youtu.be short links.
Why is Max Resolution sometimes missing?+
Not all videos have a 1280×720 maxresdefault thumbnail — older videos may only have HQ or SD versions. ThumbRip shows every size that actually exists for that video.
Is it legal to download thumbnails?+
Downloading for personal use, research, or inspiration is generally accepted. ThumbRip only accesses publicly available image URLs hosted by YouTube. Always respect the original creator's copyright.
