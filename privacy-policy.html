Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ThumbRip ("we", "us", or "our") at thumbrip.com collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website. By using ThumbRip, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information we collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use ThumbRip. However, we may automatically collect certain non-personal information when you visit our site, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • General geographic location (country/city level only)
  • IP address (anonymized)

2. How we use your information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • Monitor site performance and fix technical issues

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

3. Google AdSense and advertising

ThumbRip uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

4. Cookies

ThumbRip uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve your experience and serve relevant ads. Cookies we use include:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to show relevant ads

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our site may not function properly.

5. Third-party services

ThumbRip uses the following third-party services that may collect data subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube (img.youtube.com) — ThumbRip fetches publicly available thumbnail images hosted on YouTube's servers. We do not store these images on our servers.

6. Children's privacy

ThumbRip does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

7. Data retention

We do not store any user-submitted YouTube URLs or downloaded thumbnail images on our servers. All processing happens in your browser. We retain aggregated, anonymized analytics data to understand usage trends.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.
