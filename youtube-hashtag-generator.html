How to use YouTube hashtags effectively

YouTube allows up to 15 hashtags per video. However, using 3 to 5 highly relevant hashtags performs better than stuffing 15 generic ones. Hashtags appear clickably above your video title and help viewers find content in the same category.

For YouTube Shorts, always include #Shorts as one of your hashtags — it signals to YouTube's algorithm to distribute your content in the Shorts feed. Then add 2-3 topic-specific hashtags.

Avoid banned or overused hashtags. Focus on a mix of broad topic hashtags (e.g. #cooking) and specific ones (e.g. #italianpasta). This reaches both large and niche audiences.
